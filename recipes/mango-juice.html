Mango Juice Recipe

Unbeatable test of Mango Juice

Yellow is good for you, vitamins that are very important to your growth

Ingredients

  • Mangos
  • Sugar
  • boiled water

Steps

  1. Peel the mongoes, cut them into small blendable pieces
  2. Putting those pieces into the blender, add water and sugar to measure with the mangoes
  3. Turn on the blender, make sure the mixture is we blended
  4. Pour into a glass or cup and drink
