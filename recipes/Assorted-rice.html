Assorted Rice

The healthy aroma of Rice-assorted

Assorted rice is a delicious dish that brings together the test and sence of a well rounded balanced diet.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Carrot
  • Tomamtoes
  • Onions
  • Royco
  • Tumeric
  • French-beans

Steps

  1. First boil the rice to readiness
  2. Put Olive oil in a fresh soucepan on a slow fire for 5mins
  3. Add Onions and tomatoes to the heated oil
  4. Add royco and tumeric to the mix and stir gently
  5. Add a little water to propotion of the mix and put some salt
  6. Add the boiled rice to the mix and stir untill the rice is fully mixed
  7. Leave the mix to boil for 10 minutes
  8. Add the french-beans to the mix, cover the soucepan for 15 minutes and turn off the fire
  9. Your assorted rice is ready to serve
