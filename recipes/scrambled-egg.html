Scrambled Egg Recipe

All seasons scrambled egg

You can't go wrong with this recipe. Scrambled eggs at any day will make your mood better

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put the cooking oil on a slow fire for about 5 mins
  2. In the meantime, break the eggs and mix them together
  3. Cut the onions into small pieces and add them to the eggs
  4. Add a sprinkle of salt
  5. Put all the ingredients mixed together in the cooking oil and fry
  6. Remove th eggs when they have solidified
